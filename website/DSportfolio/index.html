About me

I'm a recent college graduate with a passion for data science. It all started with my love for computers. When I was 8 years old, I would constantly play in the safe of my grandmothers windows 8 pc. She set a password on the computer, so I was couldn't play games on it but I discovered that through safe mode I could create a new user through the admin so that I could play the Window's Pinball Game. As a lad I was fascinated by the bright lights and record keeping system of the game. I would constantly get new high scores and save them under different names to compete against myself.

Throughout school, I've always had a talent for math. It was by far my favorite subject, and I was moved up a grade in elementary school because of my proficiency. I loved that I could always find the answer and check my work just be working backwards. Compared to subjects like Art and Reading, Math made so much more sense to me, and I was far happier in that class than any other.

After High school, I was not sure what I wanted to do. I had good grades in all my subjects but nothing particularly interested me (There were no IT classes at my highschool). When I entered college, I was a Biology major because just like every other first-generation college student, my family wanted me to become a doctor. Science was cool but I had no interest in the hardcore memorization that it required. I wanted to be in a field where my mathematical creativity was appreciated.

I undeclared my major and went on search to discover what career would make me happy and bring fulfillment. I though hard about what I was good at and what I wanted to do. After only a month of thinking, and lots of visits to the deans on my university, I chose to study Information Technology.

I chose the specialty of data Science to focus my skill set on but I love the rapidly changing environment that is IT. I am pleased with my choice and haven't looked back since. I graduated in May 2022 with my Bachelors of Science in Information Technology.

Education

I graduated from Marymount University in the spring of 2022. While at Mary mount, I was a student athlete on both the Men's Basketball and Men's Track and Field teams. I served my University as a RA, Orientation Leader, and Black Student Unpin President.

During the lockdown of 2021, I took the free time I had from school to get my IBM Data Science professional Certificate. This certificate strengthens my data Science skills and equipped me with knowledge outside the classroom.

In the summer of my junior year, I took the initiative ti get my AWS Cloud Practitioner Certificate. Cloud computing has always interested me and I wanted to add some diversity to my skill set and knowledge.
